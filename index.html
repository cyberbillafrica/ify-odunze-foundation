About Us

Join Hands, Change the World

The Ify Odunze Foundation (IOF) is a nonprofit organization dedicated to breaking down barriers to education for children in rural Nigerian communities. Founded by Ifeoma Odunze, a passionate psychologist and advocate for social change, IOF is driven by the belief that every child deserves access to quality primary education, regardless of their socio-economic background. With a mission to empower young minds and foster sustainable community development, the foundation focuses on providing essential resources, infrastructure improvements, and opportunities that enable children to thrive academically and beyond.IOF’s work began with a transformative initiative at Kuje Model Primary School in Abuja, where the foundation sponsored school fees, uniforms, books, and critical upgrades like electricity and water systems for 10 deserving students. This project marked the cornerstone of IOF’s commitment to creating tangible, lasting impact in underserved areas. By addressing both immediate needs—such as learning materials—and systemic challenges, like inadequate school facilities, IOF aims to build a foundation for academic success and personal growth.Rooted in values of compassion, equity, and community, the Ify Odunze Foundation combines grassroots action with a strategic vision for scalability
